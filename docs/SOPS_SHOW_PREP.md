# SOP - Show Prep and Planning

## Purpose
Define the planning steps and checklists required before recording or streaming an episode.

## Inputs
- Episode concept, guest details, sponsor requirements, production calendar.

## Outputs
- Approved run-of-show, finalized guest info, sponsor read copy, and prep assets.

## Roles
- Producer: owns planning, schedules, and checklists.
- Host: approves topics and run-of-show.
- Guest: confirms availability, bio, and plugs.

## Checklist - Episode Planning
- [ ] Confirm episode topic and target length.
- [ ] Set recording date/time and time zone in shared calendar.
- [ ] Create run-of-show with segment timestamps and transitions.
- [ ] Collect reference links, clips, or talking points.
- [ ] Confirm sponsor slots and ad placement targets.
- [ ] Identify CTA and desired audience action.
- [ ] Create a shared folder for show assets.
- [ ] Draft episode slug/title (placeholder until final).

## Checklist - Guest Bio and Info
- [ ] Full name, preferred pronunciation, and title.
- [ ] Short bio (2-4 sentences) and long bio (optional).
- [ ] Social handles and website URLs.
- [ ] Current projects, book/album, or tour dates.
- [ ] Headshot and any approved promotional images.
- [ ] Consent for recording and usage (release form placeholder).
- [ ] Tech details: mic, camera, lighting, and internet quality.
- [ ] Backup contact method (phone/email).

## Checklist - Host Prep
- [ ] Talking points and questions finalized.
- [ ] Sponsor copy reviewed.
- [ ] Cold open and intro draft.
- [ ] Outro and CTA drafted (subscribe, website, tickets).
- [ ] Confirm wardrobe/appearance notes if video is public.
