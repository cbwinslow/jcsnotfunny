## üé¨ Production System Architecture

### Workflow Pipeline
1. **Pre-Production** ‚Üí Guest coordination, research, setup
2. **Production** ‚Üí Multi-angle recording, monitoring, backup
3. **Post-Production** ‚Üí Video editing, audio processing, quality control
4. **Distribution** ‚Üí Platform publishing, promotion, analytics
5. **Engagement** ‚Üí Community management, performance optimization

### Agent Roles & Responsibilities
- **Core Production Agent**: Orchestrate entire pipeline
- **Video Production Specialist**: Multi-camera editing, AI speaker detection
- **Audio Engineering Specialist**: Audio cleanup, sponsor integration, mastering
- **Social Media Manager**: Cross-platform posting, engagement, analytics
- **Content Distributor**: Website publishing, CDN management, SEO
- **Sponsorship Manager**: Sponsor relations, content creation, ROI tracking
- **Tour Management Specialist**: Venue booking, event promotion, logistics

### Technical Standards
- **Audio**: 48kHz/24-bit minimum, -16 LUFS target, True Peak -1.5dBTP
- **Video**: 4K premium, 1080p minimum, 60fps preferred
- **Speaker Detection**: 95%+ accuracy with AI-powered camera switching
- **Content Authenticity**: Preserve conversation integrity, natural flow

## üé≠ Content Creation Guidelines

### Episode Structure
1. **Intro** (5-10 min): Welcome, guest introduction, topic setup
2. **Main Content** (40-90 min): Deep dive, conversation flow
3. **Sponsor Segments**: 2-3 placements, natural transitions
4. **Outro** (5-10 min): Takeaways, call-to-action, next episode tease

### Quality Requirements
- **Factual Accuracy**: 99%+ accuracy with fact-checking
- **Audio Quality**: No clipping, consistent levels, clean recording
- **Video Quality**: Professional lighting, stable framing, clear audio
- **Content Authenticity**: Preserve natural conversation, minimal editing

### Sponsor Integration
- **Maximum Ad Load**: 10% of total runtime
- **Integration Style**: Natural conversation flow, host-read preferred
- **Disclosure Requirements**: Clear identification of sponsored content
- **Performance Tracking**: ROI metrics, click tracking, engagement analysis
