# SOP - Sponsor Reads, Plugs, and Disclosures

## Purpose
Standardize sponsor read handling, guest plugs, and disclosure requirements.

## Inputs
- Sponsor contract terms, ad copy, tracking links, and required disclosures.

## Outputs
- Approved ad reads, tracking links, and timestamps for ad placement.

## Checklist - Sponsor Intake
- [ ] Confirm sponsor name and product details.
- [ ] Receive approved ad copy and key points.
- [ ] Confirm required disclosures (FTC or platform specific).
- [ ] Record required links and discount codes.
- [ ] Confirm deliverables (host-read, mid-roll, end-roll).
- [ ] Confirm timeline and due dates.
- [ ] Confirm exclusion rules (no competitor mentions).

## Checklist - Sponsor Read Draft
- [ ] Write a natural host-read version of the copy.
- [ ] Confirm pronunciation of brand and product names.
- [ ] Identify where read occurs in the episode.
- [ ] Add a short CTA and link mention.
- [ ] Add fallback copy if sponsor changes last minute.
- [ ] Store final copy in a shared folder.
  - Template: `docs/templates/sponsor_read.md`

## Checklist - Guest Plugs and Dates
- [ ] Ask for 2-3 prioritized plugs (site, tour, merch).
- [ ] Collect exact URLs and handles.
- [ ] Gather upcoming dates (city, venue, date).
- [ ] Confirm any black-out dates or embargoes.
- [ ] Confirm pronunciation of venue names.

## Checklist - Post-Production Notes
- [ ] Capture timestamps for sponsor reads.
- [ ] Update show notes with links and disclosures.
- [ ] Add tracking tags to URLs as required.
