# Technical Production Standards

## Audio Engineering Absolutes

### Recording Standards
- **Sample Rate**: 48kHz minimum, 96kHz preferred for premium content
- **Bit Depth**: 24-bit minimum, 32-bit float preferred
- **Microphone Setup**: Minimum 2 mics per host, 1 per guest
- **Room Treatment**: Professional acoustic treatment required
- **Backup Recording**: Dual recording system mandatory

### Processing Requirements
- **Noise Floor**: Below -60dB for final mix
- **Target Level**: -16 LUFS for podcasts, -14 LUFS for YouTube
- **True Peak**: Maximum -1.5dBTP
- **Compression**: 2:1 ratio, 3ms attack, 100ms release for speech
- **EQ Standards**: High-pass at 80Hz, presence boost 2-4dB at 4kHz

### Quality Control
- **Phase Coherence**: All multi-mic recordings phase-aligned
- **De-essing**: Applied to all voice tracks, -6dB threshold
- **Loudness Normalization**: EBU R128 standard compliance
- **Spectral Analysis**: No clipping or digital artifacts

## Video Production Standards

### Camera Requirements
- **Resolution**: 4K (3840x2160) minimum, 8K preferred for flagship content
- **Frame Rate**: 60fps for primary cameras, 24fps for cinematic B-roll
- **Codec**: ProRes 422 HQ for editing, H.265 for delivery
- **Color Space**: Rec.709 for delivery, P3 for color grading
- **Dynamic Range**: Minimum 10 stops, 12+ preferred

### Multi-Camera Setup
- **Primary Angles**: Host 1 (profile), Host 2 (profile), Guest (center)
- **Wide Shot**: Room overview for context and reactions
- **B-Roll**: Product shots, cutaways, environment details
- **Automated Focus**: Continuous focus tracking with manual override capability

### Lighting Standards
- **Key Light**: 3-point lighting minimum
- **Color Temperature**: 5600K daylight balanced
- **Lighting Ratio**: 2:1 key-to-fill ratio
- **Background**: Controlled background with depth and branding elements

## Editing Workflow Standards

### Video Editing Pipeline
1. **Ingest & Organization**: 24-hour file organization deadline
2. **Rough Cut**: 48-hour delivery from recording date
3. **Fine Cut**: Additional 48 hours for refinement
4. **Color Grade**: Professional color correction and grading
5. **Final Export**: Master files in all required formats

### Audio Editing Pipeline
1. **Sync & Organization**: Multi-track alignment and labeling
2. **Cleanup**: Noise reduction and error correction
3. **Mixing**: Balance and EQ all tracks
4. **Mastering**: Final loudness and quality control
5. **Format Export**: Multiple format outputs

### Content Requirements
- **Segment Marking**: Clear chapter markers for long-form content
- **Lower Thirds**: Consistent branding and guest identification
- **Subtitle Quality**: 99% accuracy, proper timing, easy readability
- **Thumbnail Standards**: 1280x720, high contrast, text readable at small sizes

## File Management & Storage

### Naming Conventions
- **Episode Files**: `YYYY-MM-DD_EPXXX_GuestName_Version.mov`
- **Audio Files**: `YYYY-MM-DD_EPXXX_GuestName_TRACKNAME.wav`
- **Social Media**: `YYYY-MM-DD_EPXXX_PLATFORM_CONTENTTYPE_Version.mp4`
- **Archive**: `ARCHIVED_YYYY-MM-DD_EPXXX_Complete.zip`

### Storage Architecture
- **Hot Storage**: SSD array for active projects (minimum 10TB)
- **Cold Storage**: RAID 6 for archive (minimum 50TB)
- **Cloud Backup**: Geographic distribution, automated sync
- **Offline Backup**: LTO tapes for long-term archive

### Backup Strategy
- **3-2-1 Rule**: 3 copies, 2 media types, 1 offsite
- **Verification**: Monthly backup integrity checks
- **Recovery Testing**: Quarterly disaster recovery tests
- **Retention**: Raw footage 90 days, finals indefinite

## Quality Assurance Standards

### Technical Specifications
- **Video Quality**: No compression artifacts, smooth motion, accurate colors
- **Audio Quality**: No clipping, smooth transitions, consistent levels
- **Sync Quality**: Perfect audio-video synchronization within 1 frame
- **Metadata**: Complete and accurate for all content

### Review Process
- **Technical Review**: Engineer quality check
- **Content Review**: Producer accuracy check
- **Legal Review**: Compliance and rights verification
- **Final Approval**: Host sign-off required

### Testing Standards
- **Platform Compatibility**: Test on all target platforms
- **Device Testing**: Mobile, tablet, desktop compatibility
- **Network Testing**: Various bandwidth scenarios
- **Accessibility Testing**: Screen readers, captions, color contrast
