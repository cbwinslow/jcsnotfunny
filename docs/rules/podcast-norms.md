# Podcast Production Norms & Absolutes

## Core Foundation Principles

### 1. Production Quality Standards
- **NO COMPROMISE** on audio quality - minimum 48kHz/24-bit, -16 LUFS target
- **Video Resolution**: 4K for premium content, 1080p minimum for all distribution
- **Speaker Focus**: AI-driven camera switching with 95% accuracy on active speaker detection
- **Content Authenticity**: All edits must preserve conversation integrity and natural flow

### 2. Content Creation Rules
- **Episode Length**: 60-120 minutes for main episodes, 15-30 minutes for mini-episodes
- **Sponsor Integration**: Maximum 10% of total runtime, natural placement only
- **Guest Selection**: Must align with podcast mission and audience interests
- **Show Structure**: Consistent intro/outro format, segment-based organization

### 3. Brand Identity Absolutes
- **Visual Branding**: Consistent color scheme, logo placement, and lower thirds
- **Audio Branding**: Consistent intro/outro music, sound effects library
- **Tone of Voice**: Authentic, conversational, slightly edgy but inclusive
- **Core Values**: Truth-seeking, curiosity-driven, entertainment-focused

### 4. Technical Infrastructure
- **Backup Strategy**: 3-2-1 backup rule (3 copies, 2 media, 1 offsite)
- **Storage**: Raw footage 90 days, final content indefinite
- **Processing Pipeline**: Automated first, human reviewed second
- **Quality Control**: Multi-stage approval process required

### 5. Distribution Standards
- **Simultaneous Release**: All platforms within 30-minute window
- **Content Adaptation**: Platform-specific optimization mandatory
- **SEO Requirements**: All content must be discoverable and indexed
- **Accessibility**: Captions, transcripts, and alt-text required

### 6. Social Media Protocol
- **Posting Frequency**: Minimum 3 posts/day, maximum 8 posts/day across platforms
- **Response Time**: 2 hours for mentions, 24 hours for DMs during business hours
- **Content Mix**: 40% episode promo, 30% engaging content, 20% tour, 10% sponsor
- **Engagement Style**: Authentic, humorous, boundary-respecting

### 7. Sponsorship Guidelines
- **Brand Alignment**: Sponsors must match audience values and interests
- **Integration Style**: Host-read preferred, produced ads acceptable
- **Disclosure**: All sponsored content clearly identified
- **Performance Tracking**: ROI metrics required for all sponsorships

### 8. Tour & Event Standards
- **Venue Requirements**: Minimum capacity 500, professional AV equipment
- **Ticket Pricing**: Tiered pricing with early bird options
- **Fan Experience**: Meet & greet opportunities for VIP ticket holders
- **Promotion**: 60-day promotional cycle minimum

### 9. Community Management
- **Comment Policy**: Respectful discourse, zero tolerance for hate speech
- **Moderation Response**: 1-hour response for serious issues
- **Community Guidelines**: Clearly posted and enforced consistently
- **Fan Appreciation**: Regular community spotlights and engagement

### 10. Legal & Compliance
- **Content Ownership**: Clear rights for all music, images, and guest content
- **Privacy Protection**: GDPR compliance, personal data protection
- **Age Appropriateness**: Content warnings when necessary
- **Contract Standards**: All agreements reviewed by legal counsel

## Non-Negotiable Workflow Absolutes

### Pre-Production
1. **Guest Vetting**: Background check and values alignment required
2. **Topic Research**: Minimum 5 hours research per episode topic
3. **Equipment Check**: Full system test before every recording
4. **Backup Plan**: Redundant recording systems always active

### Production
1. **Multi-Angle Recording**: Minimum 3 camera angles, 2 audio sources
2. **Real-Time Monitoring**: Engineer monitoring levels throughout recording
3. **Segment Marking**: Live marking of key moments for editing
4. **Backup Recording**: Continuous backup to secondary system

### Post-Production
1. **First Draft**: 48-hour turnaround from recording to rough cut
2. **Review Process**: Minimum 3 approval stages required
3. **Quality Assurance**: Technical check before distribution
4. **Metadata Creation**: Complete SEO optimization required

### Distribution
1. **Simultaneous Launch**: All platforms coordinated within 30 minutes
2. **Promotional Content**: Minimum 4 social media assets per episode
3. **Analytics Setup**: Tracking pixels and UTM codes configured
4. **Archive Process**: Complete project backup within 24 hours

## Performance Metrics & KPIs

### Content Performance
- **Engagement Rate**: Minimum 3% across all platforms
- **Completion Rate**: Minimum 70% for video content
- **Share Rate**: Minimum 2% for high-quality content
- **Growth Rate**: Minimum 5% monthly follower growth

### Business Metrics
- **Sponsor ROI**: Minimum 3:1 return on ad spend
- **Merchandise Conversion**: Minimum 2% conversion rate
- **Ticket Sales**: Minimum 80% venue capacity
- **Email List Growth**: Minimum 10% monthly growth

### Quality Metrics
- **Technical Errors**: Maximum 1% error rate
- **User Complaints**: Maximum 0.5% of total interactions
- **Content Accuracy**: Minimum 99% fact-check accuracy
- **Brand Consistency**: 100% compliance with brand guidelines

## Technology & Infrastructure Standards

### Hardware Requirements
- **Cameras**: Minimum 4K capability, 60fps recording
- **Audio Interface**: 24-bit/96kHz conversion capability
- **Storage**: RAID 10 configuration, minimum 50TB capacity
- **Network**: Gigabit internet, backup connection required

### Software Standards
- **Editing Software**: Adobe Creative Cloud or approved equivalent
- **DAW**: Pro Tools or Logic Pro for audio production
- **Project Management**: Asana or Trello for task tracking
- **Communication**: Slack for internal, approved platforms for external

### Security Requirements
- **Data Encryption**: AES-256 for all sensitive data
- **Access Control**: Role-based access with 2FA required
- **Network Security**: Firewall, VPN, and intrusion detection
- **Backup Encryption**: Encrypted backup storage required
