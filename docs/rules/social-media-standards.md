# Social Media Management Rules

## Platform-Specific Standards

### Twitter/X Rules
- **Character Limits**: 280 characters standard, 420 Premium
- **Posting Frequency**: 3-8 posts/day, minimum 3 posts/week
- **Optimal Times**: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM EST
- **Hashtag Strategy**: Maximum 3 hashtags, mix trending + branded
- **Media Requirements**: 16:9 ratio for video, high-quality images
- **Engagement Protocol**: Respond to mentions within 2 hours business hours

### Instagram Rules
- **Content Mix**: 60% Reels, 25% Posts, 15% Stories
- **Posting Schedule**: 1-2 posts/day, 5-10 stories in 2-3 sessions
- **Reel Optimization**: 15-60 seconds, trending audio when appropriate
- **Hashtag Strategy**: 15-30 hashtags, mix popular/niche/location
- **Story Requirements**: Branding consistent, interactive elements
- **Engagement Standards**: Comment responses within 4 hours

### TikTok Rules
- **Video Length**: 15-60 seconds optimal, max 3 minutes
- **Content Strategy**: 40% clips, 30% trends, 20% behind scenes, 10% tour
- **Trending Audio**: Must use trending sounds when appropriate
- **Posting Time**: 7-9 PM EST peak, consistent schedule
- **Hashtag Usage**: 3-5 relevant hashtags including #podcast
- **Engagement**: Respond to comments, engage with similar content

### YouTube Rules
- **Title Optimization**: Maximum 60 characters, include episode number + guest
- **Description Standards**: Minimum 500 characters, timestamps, links
- **Thumbnail Requirements**: 1280x720, high contrast, readable text
- **Upload Schedule**: Consistent day/time, advance scheduling encouraged
- **Chapter Markers**: Timestamps for each segment
- **End Screen**: Subscribe button, next episode preview

### LinkedIn Rules
- **Professional Tone**: Industry insights, achievements, professional updates
- **Posting Frequency**: 3-5 posts/week, maximum 1 post/day
- **Content Focus**: Professional development, industry commentary
- **Article Standards**: 500-1000 words for long-form content
- **Hashtag Strategy**: 3-5 professional, industry-specific hashtags
- **Networking**: Engage with industry peers and sponsors

## Content Strategy Standards

### Content Mix Requirements
- **Episode Promotion**: 40% of total content
- **Engagement Content**: 30% of total content
- **Tour Promotion**: 20% of total content
- **Sponsor Content**: 10% of total content

### Posting Schedule Template
- **Morning (9-11 AM)**: Episode announcements, behind scenes
- **Midday (1-3 PM)**: Industry insights, professional content
- **Evening (7-9 PM)**: Entertainment content, interactive posts
- **Late Night (10 PM-12 AM)**: Community engagement, Q&A

### Content Adaptation Rules
- **Twitter**: Short, punchy, thread format for longer content
- **Instagram**: Visual-first, stories for immediacy, Reels for engagement
- **TikTok**: Trend-driven, entertainment-focused, authentic style
- **YouTube**: Educational value, comprehensive, SEO optimized
- **LinkedIn**: Professional insights, career development, industry focus

## Community Management Standards

### Response Time Requirements
- **Comments/Mentions**: 2-4 hours during business hours
- **Direct Messages**: 24 hours maximum response time
- **Negative Feedback**: 1 hour response, escalation if serious
- **Crisis Situations**: Immediate response with approved messaging

### Engagement Style Guidelines
- **Tone**: Professional yet approachable, humor appropriate
- **Controversy Handling**: Avoid unless directly relevant
- **Community Values**: Inclusive, respectful, constructive
- **Moderation**: Remove spam/inappropriate content within 1 hour

### Community Growth Strategies
- **Collaboration**: Partner with complementary creators
- **Hashtag Campaigns**: Branded hashtag for community building
- **User-Generated Content**: Encourage and feature fan content
- **Live Sessions**: Regular Q&A and interactive content

## Analytics & Performance Standards

### Key Performance Indicators
- **Engagement Rate**: Minimum 3% across all platforms
- **View-Through Rate**: Minimum 60% for video content
- **Share Rate**: Minimum 2% for high-quality content
- **Follower Growth**: Minimum 5% monthly growth
- **Conversion Rate**: Minimum 2% for promotional content

### Analytics Review Process
- **Daily Monitoring**: Real-time engagement tracking
- **Weekly Reports**: Performance analysis and optimization
- **Monthly Reviews**: Strategy adjustment based on data
- **Quarterly Deep-Dives**: Comprehensive performance analysis

### Optimization Strategies
- **A/B Testing**: Test content formats, posting times, headlines
- **Trend Monitoring**: Real-time trend identification and response
- **Competitive Analysis**: Weekly competitor content review
- **Audience Feedback**: Regular community surveys and polls

## Crisis Management Protocol

### Response Hierarchy
- **Level 1**: Minor issues, social media manager handles within 4 hours
- **Level 2**: Moderate issues, senior team review within 2 hours
- **Level 3**: Major issues, executive team response within 1 hour
- **Level 4**: Critical issues, immediate legal and PR involvement

### Communication Standards
- **Consistent Messaging**: All communication coordinated
- **Designated Spokesperson**: Approved representatives only
- **Documentation**: All incidents recorded and reviewed
- **Learning Process**: Post-crisis analysis and improvement

### Prevention Strategies
- **Content Approval**: Multi-level review for sensitive content
- **Brand Safety Filters**: Automated and manual content screening
- **Community Guidelines**: Clear rules and consistent enforcement
- **Regular Training**: Team crisis management and response training

## Content Scheduling Rules

### Advanced Planning Requirements
- **Content Calendar**: 30-day advance planning minimum
- **Batch Creation**: Content created in batches for efficiency
- **Scheduling Tools**: Automated scheduling with manual review
- **Contingency Planning**: Backup content for emergencies

### Platform-Specific Scheduling
- **Twitter**: Real-time and scheduled mix, engagement prioritized
- **Instagram**: Stories immediate, posts scheduled, Reels optimized
- **TikTok**: Trend-responsive, posting windows critical
- **YouTube**: Upload scheduling consistent, optimization timing
- **LinkedIn**: Professional hours content, industry timing relevant

### Seasonal and Event-Based Planning
- **Holiday Content**: Planned 60 days in advance
- **Tour Promotion**: 90-day promotional cycle
- **Sponsor Campaigns**: Aligned with marketing calendars
- **Industry Events**: Real-time coverage and commentary
