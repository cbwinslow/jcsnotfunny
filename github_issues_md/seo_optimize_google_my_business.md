This issue is for creating and optimizing a Google My Business (GMB) profile to enhance local search visibility for "comedy podcast roanoke, va".

**Description:**
A well-optimized GMB profile is critical for local SEO, helping the podcast appear prominently in Google Maps and local search results.

**Subtasks:**
- Create or claim the Google My Business profile for "Jared's Not Funny Podcast".
- Ensure business name, address, phone number (NAP), website, and categories (e.g., "Podcast", "Entertainment") are accurate and consistent.
- Add high-quality photos and videos to the profile.
- Write a compelling business description including relevant local keywords.
- Encourage listeners to leave positive reviews.
- Regularly post updates and events.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- A verified and fully optimized Google My Business profile is live.
- NAP information is consistent across all online platforms.
- The podcast appears in Google Maps and local search results for relevant Roanoke queries.
- Evidence of positive reviews and engagement on the GMB profile.
