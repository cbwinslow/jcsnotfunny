This issue is for developing and documenting a comprehensive keyword strategy for each episode and related content, with a specific focus on local terms relevant to "comedy podcast roanoke, va".

**Description:**
To maximize search engine visibility and target relevant local audiences, a well-defined keyword strategy is essential. This task focuses on establishing guidelines and a process for identifying and integrating primary and secondary keywords, explicitly including local geographic identifiers.

**Subtasks (from plan):**
- Develop a comprehensive keyword strategy for each episode, focusing on local terms like "comedy podcast roanoke, va" and relevant variations.
- Ensure `metadata_generator.py` includes a robust mechanism for adding episode-specific keywords.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- A documented process for keyword research and selection (including local terms) is created.
- Guidelines for keyword integration into episode titles, descriptions, and transcripts are established, with examples for local targeting.
- `metadata_generator.py` is updated to facilitate the inclusion of episode-specific keywords.
- Content creators are trained on the new local keyword strategy.
