This issue is for acquiring high-quality backlinks from other Roanoke-based websites to enhance local SEO authority.

**Description:**
Backlinks from relevant local websites signal to search engines that the podcast is an authoritative and important entity within the Roanoke community, significantly boosting local search rankings.

**Subtasks:**
- Identify relevant Roanoke-based websites (local news, blogs, community organizations, local businesses).
- Develop an outreach strategy to request backlinks or content collaborations.
- Partner with local businesses or get featured on local event calendars.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- A target number of high-quality backlinks from Roanoke-based websites are acquired.
- Increased domain authority and local search ranking for "comedy podcast roanoke, va" queries.
- Documented outreach efforts and successful backlink placements.
