This issue is for outlining and documenting strategies for acquiring high-quality backlinks to the website, with a focus on local Roanoke-based websites.

**Description:**
Backlinks from reputable sources are a critical factor for improving search engine rankings and increasing organic traffic. This task involves developing a strategic approach to acquire these valuable links, particularly emphasizing local relevance.

**Subtasks (from plan):**
- Outline backlink building strategies.
- Focus on obtaining backlinks from other Roanoke-based websites (local news, blogs, community organizations).

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- A documented strategy for identifying target websites (including local ones) and outreach methods is created.
- Guidelines for content promotion and relationship building for backlink acquisition are established.
- Progress on backlink acquisition is tracked.
