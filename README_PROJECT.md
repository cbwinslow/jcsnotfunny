# Jared's Not Funny — Project Guide

## Purpose

Support Jared with a repeatable production pipeline to turn raw podcast footage into publishable content and marketing assets.

## Roles & expectations

- Producer (you): responsible for ingest, cut, clip, QA, publishing schedule, and performance reporting.
- Host (Jared): creative direction and guest coordination.
- Contractors (optional): editors, thumbnail designers, audio engineers.

## Industry standards & deliverables (summary)

- Audio: -14 LUFS, < -1 dBTP peak, noise floor reduced
- Video: deliver masters at 1080p H.264 (10–20 Mbps), thumbnails 1280x720
- Clips: 15–90s, optimized for each platform format; captions included
- Turnaround: full episode 3–7 days (MVP), clips within 24–48 hours after final cut

## KPIs

- Views / plays per episode
- Click-throughs to website
- Conversion: mailing list / merch / ticket sales
- Engagement: likes, comments, shares (platform broken down)
