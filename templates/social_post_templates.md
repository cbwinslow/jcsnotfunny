# Social Post Templates

Use these templates when creating social posts. Replace placeholders: {title}, {guest}, {ep_link}, {date}, {short_link}

X / Twitter (280 chars):
"{title} — new episode with {guest} • Watch: {ep_link} • {short_link} #JaredsNotFunny"

Instagram (Caption):
"{title}\nGuest: {guest}\n\nListen on YouTube (link in bio) or at jaredsnotfunny.com/episodes/{slug}\n\n#podcast #comedy"

YouTube Description (Episode):
"{title}\nGuest: {guest}\n\nFull episode: {ep_link}\nChapters: {timestamps}\n\nSubscribe: https://www.youtube.com/channel/{{CHANNEL_ID}}\nWebsite: https://jaredsnotfunny.com/episodes/{slug}\n"

YouTube Short (Description):
"Clip from {title} w/ {guest} — Watch the full episode: {ep_link}"
